Web Audio
Mixer
This is a simple mixer built with the Web Audio API. The mixer has seven channels each with individual volume, track pan, muting, and 3-band EQ. There is also a master volume fader. I used open source stems for "Don't Stop 'til You Get Enough" by Michael Jackson for the channel audio.
