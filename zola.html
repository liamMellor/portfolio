The Everywhere
Store Dashboard
I built version 1 of The Everywhere Store customer CMS dashboard while I was an intern at Zola Books. The app is built on Angular with Boostrap and is built to consume Zola Book's internal API, 'Janus'. It is used to administer over the Zola customer base and perform basic actions like editing user information and managing purchases.
